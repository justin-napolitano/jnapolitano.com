### Restrictions
* Must be near a pipeline terminal

### Imports 

* Pipeline Data
* Well Data

### Filtering

* For each well calculate nearest pipeline

* For each well calculate geographic distance from pipeline

* eliminate wells further than 2 km from a pipeline
